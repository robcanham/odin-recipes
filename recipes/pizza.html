Pizza Recipe

How to make pizza

a short, inaccurate recipe for making pizza. I'd recommend just giving Dominos or Pizza Hut a call.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Sauce
  • Cheese
  • Oven

Steps:

  1. Roll the Dough
  2. Add the sauce
  3. Add the cheese
  4. Cook at 180C for 12-15 minutes
  5. Eat the pizza
